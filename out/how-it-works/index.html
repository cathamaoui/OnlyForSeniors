How Only For Seniors Works

Whether you're 65 or 95, our directory is built to be simple.

1. Search or browse

Type what you need into the big search bar, or tap a colour-coded category that interests you.

2. Pick a business

Every listing shows a description, hours, photos, and reviews. Look for the green ✓ Verified badge.

3. Tap to call

Press the big orange button to call them right from your phone. Or tap the green button to send a message.

4. Get help

The business replies, books your service, or answers your questions. It&apos;s that simple.

5. Leave a review

Help other seniors by sharing your experience. Honest reviews keep our community safe.
